Quiz to check your knowledge

Which of the following herbs is commonly used in Ayurveda for promoting digestion and balancing the Pitta dosha?

Ginger is known for its digestive properties and is widely used in Ayurveda to balance the Pitta dosha.

Which Ayurvedic herb is considered a powerful adaptogen and is used to help the body cope with stress and fatigue?

Ashwagandha is a well-known adaptogen in Ayurveda, helping the body manage stress and improve energy levels.

Which herb in Ayurveda is known for its ability to detoxify the liver and support overall immune health?

Guduchi (also known as Giloy) is praised in Ayurveda for its detoxifying properties and its role in boosting immunity and supporting liver health.Guduchi (also known as Giloy) is praised in Ayurveda for its detoxifying properties and its role in boosting immunity and supporting liver health.

Which Ayurvedic herb is commonly used to improve memory and cognitive function, and is known as "Brahmi" in Sanskrit?

Brahmi (Bacopa monnieri) is widely used in Ayurveda to enhance memory, concentration, and overall cognitive function.

Which of the following herbs is known in Ayurveda for its anti-inflammatory properties and is often used to support joint health?

Turmeric, especially its active compound curcumin, is renowned in Ayurveda for its powerful anti-inflammatory and healing properties, often used to support joint health.

Which herb is traditionally used in Ayurveda to balance the Vata dosha and promote relaxation and sleep?

Jatamansi is an Ayurvedic herb known for its calming effects, helping to balance the Vata dosha and promote better sleep and relaxation.
